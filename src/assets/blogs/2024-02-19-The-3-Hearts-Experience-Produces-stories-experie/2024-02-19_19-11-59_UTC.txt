The 3 Hearts Experience:
Produces stories/ experiences in order to help our viewers to escape away from real life for an hour or two. All of our stories at 3 Hearts Experience stories have a larger meaning and an overall goal and lesson that they are trying to teach the viewer. Also, each screenplay is associated with a specific cause that the money generated from the screenplay gets donated to.
