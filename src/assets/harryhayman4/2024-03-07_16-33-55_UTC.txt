Big congratulations to Rachel Branson on her appointment as Director of the Office of Minority Business Success in #Philadelphia!

With over 17 years of experience in various legal roles, including her recent tenure as General Counsel & Corporate Secretary for Nymbus, Inc., Rachel brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to this crucial role.

Rachel's dedication to supporting minority businesses and fostering economic growth is truly commendable. I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to work alongside her as she helps minority entrepreneurs launch and thrive in our city.

In collaboration with the Office of Economic Opportunity and the Department of Commerce, Rachel will spearhead initiatives to ensure diverse business owners have the resources and support they need to succeed. Together, we'll strive to achieve measurable goals for small, medium, and large business growth across Philadelphia.

Congratulations, Rachel! Your leadership and vision will make a significant impact on our community, and I can't wait to see the positive changes we'll achieve together.
