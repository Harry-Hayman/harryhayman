Great cause, good people all at The Great Chefs Saturday For Alex's Lemonade Stand.  Alex’s Lemonade Stand Foundation is one of the leading funders of childhood cancer research in the U.S. Alex's Lemonade Stand Program.⁣
More than 30 superstar chefs from across the country came together under one roof and served their signature tasting dishes at this family-friendly event. And the cherry on top: this year marks The 15th Annual Great Chefs Event Philadelphia!⁣
We had the chance to taste delectable food, bid on fabulous auction items, and celebrate 15 years of this amazing event alongside their favorite chefs. Join the movement to end pediatric cancer by supporting and helping fight for a cure!⁣
