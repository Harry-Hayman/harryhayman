Our #Philadelphia neighbors are not able to afford to eat.

The cost of food has gone up, but the support for those who can’t afford it has not.

Philadelphia is among the worst of all US cities with food insecurity problems, and it also receives the least amount of government support.

“I Am Hungry” is getting to the bottom of this issue, and uncovers the real people who are suffering… many of which are not who you expect.
