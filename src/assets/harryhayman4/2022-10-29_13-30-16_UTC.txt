October 27th was the third annual Black Entrepreneurs Day.  Black Entrepreneurs across the country applied for the chance to win $25,000 each to help grow and scale their businesses.  Everyone gathered to celebrate Black businesses and entrepreneurship winners of the NAACP Powershift Entrepreneur Grant $250,000 live from Harlem's Legendary Apollo Theater.⁣
Congratulations to everyone who applied and received the $25,000 to Scale Their Business.⁣
