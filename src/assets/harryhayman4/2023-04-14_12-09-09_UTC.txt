 Mark Edwards is President & CEO of The Food Trust; Mark provides strategic oversight of the organization and works to optimize its capacity in the areas of finance, operations, and human capital.⁣
@TheFoodTrust works with neighborhoods, institutions, retailers, farmers, and policymakers across the country to ensure delicious, nutritious food for all. Backed by three decades of research and evaluation, our holistic community-centered approach to nutrition security weaves together three core programming elements — access, affordability, and education — as well as a focus on advocating for public policy solutions.⁣
